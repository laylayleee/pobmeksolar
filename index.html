WHO IS PERTHSANTA?

Two distinct paths, one powerful universe. Perth Tanapon and Santa Pongsapak form one of the most dynamic and fiercely talented duos in the Thai entertainment industry.

Individually, they have proven themselves as versatile artists. Together, their magnetic on-screen chemistry and shared passion for storytelling have created an unstoppable force.

PERTH TANAPON

Born: March 20, 2001

Stepping into the spotlight around 2017, Perth quickly rose to international fame. Known for his ability to convey deep, complex emotions, he is a multi-talented artist, gifted musician, singer, and guitarist. He is currently gearing up for his highly anticipated solo projects, Scarlet Heart and High & Low.

SANTA PONGSAPAK

Born: November 5, 2003

Santa burst onto the scene capturing hearts with his phenomenal dance skills and highly expressive acting. Known for his incredible versatility and natural ability to bring deep emotional resonance to his characters, he truly lights up the screen with every performance. Beyond his outstanding acting prowess, he is also a talented performer and a proud member of the beloved T-Pop group Jasper.
